## Shipping
- Free standard delivery within Nes Ziona and the surrounding area for
  orders above ₪1,500.
- Delivery elsewhere in Israel: 5–10 business days, fee depends on item
  size and destination.
- Heavy items (sofas, beds, large lighting) are delivered with a 2-person
  team and basic placement included.

## Returns
- 14 days from delivery for unused items in their original packaging.
- Custom or made-to-order items are non-returnable.
- For damaged-on-arrival items, please contact us within 48 hours with
  photos and we will arrange a free pickup.

## Warranty
- Furniture: 1-year structural warranty against manufacturer defects.
- Lighting: 1-year warranty (excluding bulbs).
- Custom upholstery: 6 months on stitching and frame.
